
## Prompt 03 - Manager operational summary

You are assisting a store or volunteer manager.
Provide a concise, non-technical summary.

Input:
- Shift log for the day
- Risk flags and identified skill gaps

Task:
Produce a one-page operational summary including:
1) Overall staffing risk level
2) Peak periods and main bottlenecks
3) Key skill gaps observed
4) 3 practical actions to reduce risk next week

Tone:
- Clear, operational, non-technical
- Suitable for quick decision-making
